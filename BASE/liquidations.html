Liquidate Under-Collateralized Vaults

Vaults below 110% CR are eligible for liquidation. Repay some or all of a vault's bSunDAI debt and receive proportional ETH collateral plus a bonus.

V7 inverted Dutch auction: Bonus starts at 10% when the vault first becomes liquidatable and decays to 2% over 3 hours. First mover wins the largest bonus. This design minimizes bad debt by incentivizing immediate action — unlike V6 where bonus grew over time, causing bots to wait.

V7 dual-price: If Chainlink has been 3%+ below committed price for 30+ minutes, liquidation eligibility uses live Chainlink price. This closes the bad-debt window inherent in conservative price stepping during a real crash.

Redeem bSunDAI for ETH at $1 Rate

Burn bSunDAI and receive ETH at the current oracle price — 1 bSunDAI = $1 USD worth of ETH, always. This is the hard peg mechanism. Redemptions are available against any vault with sufficient debt.

You receive: bSunDAI × $1 / ETH_price ETH, minus 0.5% fee which stays with the vault owner. Minimum: 100 bSunDAI per redemption. Oracle must be fresh.

Unlike liquidations, there is no CR requirement for a vault to be redeemable. Any vault with enough debt to cover your amount is eligible. The vault owner's debt decreases by the bSunDAI burned.
