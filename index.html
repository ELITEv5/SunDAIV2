How pSunDAI Works

1. What is pSunDAI?

pSunDAI is a PLS-collateralized stable asset on PulseChain. Lock WPLS, mint pSunDAI (targeting $1 USD). No admin, no governance, fully autonomous once deployed.

2. Deposit PLS → Mint pSunDAI

Deposit native PLS directly (auto-wraps to WPLS). Minimum 150% collateral ratio to mint. Auto mode mints at 155% in one transaction.

3. Collateral Ratio (CR)

CR = (PLS collateral × PLS price) ÷ pSunDAI debt × 100%. Stay above 150% to mint, above 110% to avoid liquidation. Target 175%+ for safety.

4. Oracle: PulseX 5-Pool TWAP + Spot

  • TWAP (Conservative): 5-pool PulseX median with asymmetric stepping — slow to fall (4h confirmation), fast to rise (30min). Used for minting and withdrawal safety checks.
  • Spot Median: Real-time 5-pool price. Used for liquidation eligibility when a real crash is confirmed.
  • Spot Warning Track: If spot stays 5%+ below TWAP for 30 continuous minutes, spot liquidation activates. Flash crashes clear in minutes; real crashes don't.

5. Liquidations

Vaults below 110% CR can be liquidated. Bonus grows from 2% to 5% over 3 hours after the auction clock starts. Call markUndercollateralized(addr) to start the clock, then liquidate later for higher bonus. See Vault Dashboard to scan and liquidate.

6. Repay & Withdraw

Repay pSunDAI to reduce debt. Withdraw PLS as native or WPLS. 5-minute cooldown after deposit. "Repay All & Withdraw" closes position in one transaction.

7. Spot Liquidation (Dual-Price)

If the spot warning has been active for 30+ minutes, liquidations use real-time spot price instead of conservative TWAP. A flash crash lasts minutes — spot recovers before 30 min, no liquidations triggered. A real crash stays down — spot confirms after 30 min.

8. Emergency Functions

  • Emergency Unlock: Zero debt + last deposit older than 30 days → withdraw regardless of oracle.
  • Emergency Repay / Withdraw: If oracle is dead for 7+ days, repay and withdraw without price checks.

9. Protocol Tools (anyone can call)

  • Reconcile: Net surplus buffer against bad debt on-chain.
  • Poke: Advance oracle state machine (rate-limited to once per 30 min).
  • Clear Bad Debt: Seize underwater vault — caller keeps all PLS free.
  • Settle Debt: Burn your pSunDAI to cancel accumulated bad debt.
